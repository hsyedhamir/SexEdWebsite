Team
Sofia Marcone
UX Research/UI
After noticing a deficit in reliable and diverse sex education, Sofia came up with the idea of SexSupport. She led the team to create what she envisioned for an inclusive site for all bodies.
Racheal Arewa
UX Research
The main researcher, Racheal interviewed and analyzed what was missing in today’s sexual education and what people are looking for in a sex ed website.
Hussaini Syedhamir
